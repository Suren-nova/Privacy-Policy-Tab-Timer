Introduction

Welcome to the Task Timer Extension! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your privacy.

Information We Collect

The Task Timer Extension collects the following information:

  • Tab Data: We track which tabs you are working on, including the time spent on each tab. This information is used to provide timer notifications and improve your experience with the extension.
  • Storage: We store your preferences, such as the set time for each tab, in Chrome’s local storage to ensure that the settings persist across sessions.
  • Notifications: We use Chrome's notification API to send alerts when the timer is up. No personal data is shared with third-party services for notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected to:

  • Provide accurate and timely notifications about your tab’s usage.
  • Allow the extension to work as intended by storing user settings locally.
  • Improve the performance and user experience of the extension.

Data Sharing

We do not share any personal or identifiable information with third parties. Your information is used solely within the Task Timer Extension for functionality and performance purposes.

Cookies

The Task Timer Extension does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. All data is stored locally on your browser and not sent to any external servers.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
